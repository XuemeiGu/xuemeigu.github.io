## Overview

I develop intelligent systems that support and increasingly automate parts of scientific discovery. This work combines large language models, multi-agent workflows, scientific tools, and structured knowledge to help generate ideas, reason about research questions, and identify promising directions.

## Current research

- autonomous and multi-agent workflows for quantum physics research
- language-model agents with access to specialized scientific tools
- knowledge graphs for mapping emerging topics and hidden connections
- scientific idea generation, evaluation, and human–AI collaboration
