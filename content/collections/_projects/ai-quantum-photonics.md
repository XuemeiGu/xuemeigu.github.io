## Overview

I develop and use computational methods that help discover quantum optical experiments and photonic protocols. The goal is to move from manual trial-and-error design toward systems that can search large experimental spaces, propose candidate setups, and expose the physical ideas behind the solutions.

## Focus

- automated design of photonic quantum experiments
- interpretable representations of optical setups and quantum states
- AI-assisted discovery of new mechanisms in quantum optics
- human-in-the-loop refinement of machine-generated proposals
