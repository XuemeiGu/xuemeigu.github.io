## Overview

This research direction studies how structured light, indistinguishability, interference, and photonic circuits can be used to create and manipulate quantum states. It connects foundational questions in quantum optics with experimentally motivated designs for quantum technologies.

## Focus

- high-dimensional and multipartite photonic entanglement
- quantum interference with structured photons
- path identity and indistinguishability as design resources
- photonic protocols for quantum information processing
