## Overview

Scientific literature contains evolving patterns of concepts, collaborations, and research directions. I use knowledge graphs and related machine-learning methods to study how ideas emerge, connect, and become impactful across scientific fields.

## Focus

- forecasting high-impact scientific topics
- modeling evolving concept networks
- discovering hidden links between research areas
- building tools that support scientific creativity
